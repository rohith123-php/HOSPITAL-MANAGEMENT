# HOSPITAL-MANAGEMENT
1. 🧑‍⚕️ Patient Module
Designed to give patients control over their healthcare journey.

Registration & Login: Secure account creation and authentication.
Book Appointments: Patients can browse available doctors and book consultations.
Dashboard: A dedicated portal to view appointment statuses (Pending, Approved, Completed) and access their medical history.
View Prescriptions: Patients can view and download prescriptions and medical notes provided by doctors.
2. 🩺 Doctor Module
Designed to help medical professionals manage their daily schedule efficiently.

Secure Login: Access restricted to authorized medical staff.
Manage Appointments: Doctors can view their upcoming schedules and check patient details before the consultation.
Add Prescriptions: Provide digital prescriptions and medical notes directly to the patient's portal after an appointment.
3. 🛡️ Admin Module
The central control hub for hospital management.

Secure Login: Administrative access to oversee the system.
Manage Entities: Full control to add, edit, or remove Doctors and Patients from the system.
Approve Appointments: Review pending appointment requests and approve or reschedule them based on doctor availability.
Generate Reports: (Upcoming feature) Overview of hospital statistics, patient flow, and doctor performance.
🚀 Future Enhancements (Mini-Project Scope)
To further modernize the system, the following features are planned for future updates:

QR Code Integration: Quick patient identification using scannable QR codes.
Email Notifications: Automated alerts for appointment confirmations and prescription updates.
Online Payment Gateway: Seamless billing and consultation fee processing.
Live Bed Availability: Real-time tracking of hospital bed occupancy
